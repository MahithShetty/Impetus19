BUSINESS AS USUAL

Business and management are the quintessential requirements for any budding engineer looking to spread out wings in a vast market filled with opportunities in the core field or that related to marketing. BUSSINESS AS USUAL is a three round process which tests all the aforementioned qualities in an engineer.

First round is a questionnaire for the business minded individuals. It involves critical thinking questions and checks the ability to choose the correct answer in an array of contextually similar answers. Those who obtain a minimum threshold of marks will get qualified for the second round

Second round requires the participant to analyze the given situation, tricky in its nature. The participants must provide a solution to the problem on a piece of paper as an individual perspective. The participants must come together to a common consensus. The most convincing solution gets qualified for the next round.

THE GRAND FINALE - it tests the ultimate skill of marketing and properly analyzing a product. The participants are required to convince the judges (buyers) into buying the product (hypothetical). NO REAL TRANSACTIONS ARE INVOLVED. The participant must be able to properly provide an explanation about the product looking in to the positives of the product and change the kind of the buyer to buy the product. The participants will be open to a round of cross questioning by the judges aka buyers. The most eloquent seller will be crowned supreme and shall be considered as a stewed businessman.

Dare Devil

Round 1: Blank Coding with a normal keyboard. Participant will be given a basic coding question (something like reversing a string or sorting an array). The participant will have to code that on C while he/she is blindfolded. Depending on the number of people participating, the elimination: eligible for next round ratio will be maintained

Round 2: The previous solution will have to be typed again, but this time the layout of the keyboard is changed from QWERTY to ABCDE. The monitor will be off but the participant will not be blindfolded

Round 3: The participants who're at the top in 2nd round will progress to the 3rd round. They’ll be blindfolded and we'll dictate few easy sentences which they have to type on the ABCDE layout keyboard.

All the rounds will have a time limit (less than 20 minutes for each round). They'll be judged on per keystroke basis, each wrong keystroke or if not finished in time, the remaining letters to be typed add 5 negative points, the participant with least negative points wins.
